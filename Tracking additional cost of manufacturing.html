Tracking Additional Cost of Manufacturing

To track Additional Costs of manufacturing, create a Manufacturing Journal with BoM in the manner, as explained in the previous section. When you manufacture a product, you have to consider certain other costs like water charges, wages and electricity in addition to the cost of the component, which are added to the product cost during the process of manufacturing. In Tally.ERP 9, you can record additional costs in % or as notional value to the product cost, while entering a manufacturing Journal.
